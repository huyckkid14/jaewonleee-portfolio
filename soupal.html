Ryan Soupal named new assistant principal of Clague

By Jaewon Lee
View on The Cougar Star
Ryan Soupal joins the Clague team after previously working at Scarlett Middle School.
Photo by Yuri Kim

Clague Middle School is excited to welcome Ryan Soupal as its new assistant principal. With 23 years of experience in education, Soupal brings a wealth of knowledge and a passion for working with diverse students and staff.

Soupal’s career in education is marked by a commitment to fostering student growth and supporting educational communities. His experience includes various leadership roles, which he believes have prepared him for this position at Clague.

“I think getting to work with such a diverse group of students and staff excites me the most,” Soupal said. “It gives you the ability to learn about lots of different people and work with really great students and staff here at Clague.”

His enthusiasm for diversity reflects the core values of Clague Middle School, where different backgrounds and perspectives contribute to a rich learning environment.

“I aim to continue to serve in this role and serve this community, and to become the biggest supporter of the Clague Cougars,” he said.

Clague parent Sara-Beth Badalamente worked with him over the summer at the Summer Learning Institute.

“I’ve seen firsthand the connections Mr. Soupal makes with students,” Badalamente said. “He wants kids to succeed and he knows how to make an environment for students to be their best selves.”

Contrary to student belief, Soupal did not teach at Scarlett Middle School.

“I worked as the International Baccalaureate coordinator, helping Scarlett become an authorized International Baccalaureate World School,” Soupal said.

“I felt like becoming the assistant principal of Clague was the next step in my educational and professional journey,” Soupal said.

“Getting to meet all of the great students and staff here is my favorite part,” he said.

“I am very excited to know that Mr. Soupal will be our new assistant principal,” Benqiao Lin said.

With his dedication and extensive experience, Clague Middle School anticipates positive developments under Soupal’s leadership.
