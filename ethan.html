Cougars of Clague: Ethan Jeong

By Jaewon Lee
View on The Cougar Star
Seventh grader Ethan Jeong posing for a photo.
Courtesy of Jeong.

Q: Tell us a little about yourself.
A: “I’m Ethan Jeong. I’ve lived here since I was born, and I have a little sister named Ian who is one year apart from me.”

Q: What is your favorite class and why?
A: “Spanish, because Ms. Jakubowski is my favorite Spanish teacher.”

Q: What hobbies/sports/clubs do you do and why do you like them?
A: “I like to make Crafts with all the DIY stuff in my own room.”

Q: What is something you are proud of?
A: “I am proud to live in a place full of people that I knew since Kindergarten, and new friends since 6th grade. I am proud to be who I am!”

Q: What’s an interesting/unique fact about yourself?
A: “I care about people who are injured, people that are new to Clague, and people that I talk with. I care about people in CMS.”
