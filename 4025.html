Dr. Vehlin Doh’s Team Discovers Newly Excavated “Transition Era”

By Jaewon Lee
Image of Vehlin Doh and her team excavating the so-called “Chromebook” and “Starbucks.”
Photo by Jaewon Lee

Editor’s note: What we once called “normal life” is now ancient history—this report invites readers of 4025 to glimpse the strange, screen-filled struggles of early digital civilization.

A team of historians and archaeologists announced Monday that they have formally designated a newly uncovered stretch of history, spanning from the early 21st century to the 22nd century as a distinctive historical period, now officially named the Transition Era.
