Reflection

I chose this article because it showcases a variety of AI uses in one of the most revered universities in Michigan. This topic is important because it explains about a new change at a college that many Huron students will probably go to. It shows how journalism can inform the community even if it is not informational for certain.

This piece demonstrates my ability to interview people, as I was able to get a few quotes from a very high-ranking person in the university. It also shows my strength in organization. I made sure the article was clear and informative for my audience.

One challenge I faced was organizing all the quotes I had for the article. I overcame this by dumping everything out and rearranging it bit by bit. This helped make the organization more interesting and natural.
